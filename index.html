Syntax

Order of registers: Rd, Rs, Rt
Refer to registers with their numbers (0..7).
In lw and sw: Register goes before memory address.
Put labels between parentheses.
Lead comments with # or //.
                
Example:
addi 2 1 5  # adds r1 with immediate 5 and stores in r2.
(end)       # label
j end       # jump to label
